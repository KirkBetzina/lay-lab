# THE LAY LAB - CONTENT STRATEGY (EDUCATIONAL FOCUS)

## BRAND POSITIONING

**Key Shift:** This is NOT a betting service. It's an educational platform teaching people about horse racing markets, data analysis, and betting exchange mechanics.

**Core Message:** "Here's how I analyze horse racing — you decide what to do with the information."

---

## EDUCATIONAL PILLARS

### Pillar 1: Understanding Lay Betting (Weeks 1-2)
- What is a lay bet?
- How betting exchanges work (Betfair, etc.)
- Why laying favorites is mathematically sound
- Bankroll management fundamentals
- Risk/reward ratios explained

### Pillar 2: Data & Analysis (Weeks 3-4)
- What metrics matter in horse racing
- How to read form guides
- Why odds movement matters
- Introduction to AI/data-driven analysis
- Building your own selection framework

### Pillar 3: Practical Application (Weeks 5+)
- How to set up a betting exchange account
- Record-keeping methods
- Common mistakes and how to avoid them
- Building a sustainable approach

## POSTING FRAMEWORK

### Every Post Must Answer:
1. What concept am I teaching?
2. Why does it matter?
3. Where can they learn more?

### Post Structure (Educational):
- Hook: Pose a question
- Body: Explain the concept with data/examples
- CTA: "DM 'GUIDE' for my free beginner's course" or "Save this for later"
