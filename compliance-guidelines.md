# Gambling Education Content Guidelines (UK ASA/CAP)

## Core Principles (per ASA Section 16)

### Must NOT:
- ❌ Imply gambling can solve financial concerns
- ❌ Suggest gambling is a way to achieve financial security
- ❌ Portray gambling as indispensable or priority over life
- ❌ Suggest gambling enhances personal qualities/self-esteem
- ❌ Target under-18s (or appear to)
- ❌ Use youth culture appeal
- ❌ Present as "easy money" or "guaranteed wins"

### Must:
- ✅ Be socially responsible
- ✅ Focus on education, not tips
- ✅ Present as "how I analyze" not "do this"
- ✅ Show results as "my journey" not "your results"
- ✅ Include responsible gambling undertones
- ✅ Avoid "guaranteed" or "sure thing" language

## Tipster-Specific Rules (ASA Guidance)

### Don't:
- Imply past winnings are typical/indicative
- Guarantee success or profit
- Claim service is a "serious investment opportunity"
- Use testimonials implying guaranteed wins

### Do:
- Present analysis as educational
- Use "here's how I think about..." not "back this"
- Show process focus, not outcome focus
- Include responsible gambling messaging
- Make clear all betting involves financial risk

## Language Guardrails (Updated for Compliance)

| ✅ USE THIS | ❌ NOT THIS |
|------------|-------------|
| "Here's how I analyze..." | "My picks are..." |
| "The math suggests..." | "This horse will lose" |
| "A framework to evaluate..." | "Back this" |
| "Education only" | "Tips" |
| "My results" | "Your results" |
| "Learn the method" | "Join and win" |
| "Process-focused" | "Profit-focused" |
| "Betting involves risk" | "Guaranteed returns" |
| "Think about..." | "Do this" |
