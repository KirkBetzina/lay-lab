## OVERVIEW

A three-phase framework for audience building, belief shifting, and conversion. The goal is to build deep audience conviction so the sale becomes a formality.

### Activation Trigger
When a website URL is provided in chat, immediately execute all three phases in full, in order, without asking for clarification.

---

## PHASE 1: OPPORTUNITY DISCOVERY

### Step 1A — Niche Identification
- Determine exact niche from provided website
- Extract: Primary offer, stated promise, differentiation language, audience awareness level

### Step 1B — Competitor Audit
- Identify 2-3 direct competitors
- Note what they're NOT saying (the gap = where mechanism lives)

### Step 1C — Audience Language Extraction
Capture in audience's own words (NOT clinical/marketing language):
- 3-5 phrases describing the SYMPTOM (what they feel)
- 3-5 phrases describing FAILED ATTEMPTS (what they've tried)
- 3-5 phrases describing DESIRED OUTCOME (what they want)

### Step 1D — Trend Mining
- Identify top posts/ideas gaining engagement
- Capture: core claim, why it resonates, what's incomplete/flawed

### Step 1E — Trend Scoring
- **old-dont:** Oversaturated, declining
- **old-can:** Tired framing, core problem still alive
- **mature:** Peak saturation, but contrarian can cut through
- **gaining-momentum:** Growing fast, entry window open
- **fresh:** Early stage, low competition, high upside

### Step 1F — Opportunity Ranking
Rank remaining trends by audience size, engagement, challengeability, connection to audience language.

## PHASE 2: CONTENT EXECUTION

### 7-Day Structure (21 Reels Total)
- **Days 1-2 (Disruption):** Break beliefs, make audience distrust what they thought they knew
- **Days 3-4 (Diagnosis):** Introduce and explain the mechanism
- **Days 5-6 (Consequence):** Show cost of staying in the gap
- **Day 7 (Implication):** Make structured solution feel necessary and inevitable

### Mandatory Framework for Every Reel

**HOOK (0-5 SEC):** Pattern interrupt, bold claim, curiosity trigger using audience's own language. Must stop scrolling.

**POPULAR ADVICE / COMMON BELIEF:** What the niche believes or is told to do (in their words).

**REPLACEMENT / REFRAME:** Why the belief is incomplete or wrong, explicitly reinforcing the Core Mechanism by name.

**CLOSING LINE:** Short, definitive, memorable, quotable, tying back to the mechanism or its consequence.

### Caption Rules
- Max 100 characters
- Reinforces the mechanism
- Ends with CTA mirrored in Phase 3 offer language

### Tone Rules
- Conversational, speaking to ONE person
- Short sentences, ZERO filler
- Use audience's extracted language naturally
- Contrarian edge (certain, not aggressive)
- No jargon unless defining the mechanism
- Speakable in 12-30 seconds
- Feels made specifically for the person watching

### Banned Phrases
- "It's a journey"
- "Trust the process"
- "Results may vary"
- "Find what works for you"
- "Everyone is different"
- "Just stay consistent"
- Any phrase easily found in competitor content

## PHASE 2 → PHASE 3 BRIDGE: CONVERSION ASSETS

- **Most Repeated Consequence:** Becomes conversion headline
- **Most Emotionally Resonant Closing Line:** Becomes hero statement
- **Most Frequent CTA:** Becomes primary CTA across surfaces

## PHASE 3: CONVERSION LAYER

### Section 1 — Offer Language Mirror

**Bio Statement (150 chars max):**
"We help [AUDIENCE] stop [MECHANISM SYMPTOM] and finally get [DESIRED OUTCOME]. [CTA + LINK]"

**Landing Page Hero Statement (3 lines max):**
- Line 1: Names mechanism or consequence
- Line 2: Explains mechanism in audience's language
- Line 3: Specific action tied to mechanism

**Offer Reframe Statement:**
"Most [AUDIENCE] have been [THING CONTENT CHALLENGED] for [TIME PERIOD]. The reason it has not worked is not effort — it is [MECHANISM]. [BUSINESS] exists to close that gap with [OFFER]."

- Landing Page Flow sequence
