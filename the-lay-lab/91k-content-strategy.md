# THE LAY LAB - CONTENT STRATEGY FRAMEWORK (91K)
## A 3-Phase Execution Document

**Purpose:** This framework is designed to be adapted for ANY business's content strategy. Use it to structure educational, compliant content — then customize the pillars and language to fit the specific business.

---

## UNDERSTANDING THE FRAMEWORK

This is NOT a business-specific strategy. It's a TEMPLATE showing how to:
1. Position your brand as educational (not a tipster/service)
2. Build content pillars that teach rather than sell
3. Use language that complies with advertising regulations
4. Create a content calendar from scratch

**Phase 1:** Foundation (What & Why)
**Phase 2:** Application (How To)
**Phase 3:** Integration (Deep Dive)

---

## PHASE 1: EDUCATIONAL PILLARS

### What Are They?
Educational pillars are the MAIN topics your content will teach. Each pillar becomes a content category.

### How To Build Yours:
1. Identify your core topic
2. Break it into teachable concepts
3. Structure from beginner to advanced

### Framework Example:
- **Pillar 1:** [Core Concept] — "Understanding the basics"
- **Pillar 2:** [Data & Analysis] — "How to evaluate like a pro"
- **Pillar 3:** [Practical Application] — "Building your own system"

---

## PHASE 2: CONTENT THEME SWAP

### Why It Matters
Your current content may be salesy or non-compliant. This shows how to REFRAME it educationally.

### Framework:

| OLD (Salesy/Non-Compliant) | NEW (Educational) |
|---------------------------|-------------------|
| "My picks for Monday" | "What makes a good candidate — here's my framework" |
| "Back this horse" | "The math behind why favorites often lose" |
| "Guaranteed wins" | "Process-focused results" |

### How To Apply To Your Business:
1. Take each piece of OLD content
2. Identify what concept it teaches
3. Reframe as "Here's how I analyze..."
4. Always end with CTA to learn more

---

## PHASE 3: POSTING FRAMEWORK

### Every Post Must Answer:
1. What concept am I teaching?
2. Why does it matter?
3. Where can they learn more?

### Post Structure:
- **Hook:** Pose a question
- **Body:** Explain the concept with data/examples
- **CTA:** "DM [keyword] for free guide" or "Save for later"

## LANGUAGE GUARDRAILS

### Framework Rules:

| Say This | Not This |
|----------|----------|
| "Here's how I analyze..." | "My picks are..." |
| "The math suggests..." | "This will win" |
| "A framework to evaluate..." | "Back this" |
| "Education only" | "Tips" |
| "My results" | "Your results" |
| "Learn the method" | "Join and win" |
| "Process-focused" | "Profit-focused" |

---

## CTA PLACEMENT

### Framework Rules:
- **End of carousel:** "Save this for later — Part X tomorrow"
- **End of reels:** "Follow for more on [topic]"
- **Bio:** "[Keyword] education | Sharing my journey | DM '[keyword]' for free guide"

### Soft CTA to paid (optional):
- "If you want a structured course, link in bio"
- "Join the community to discuss with fellow learners"
- NEVER: "Join now to win" or "Guaranteed results"

---

## COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

### Framework Must-Haves:
✅ Every post framed as educational
✅ No specific recommendations framed as advice
✅ "How I do it" not "Do this"
✅ Results shown as "my journey" not "proven"
✅ Always suggest independent research
✅ No "guaranteed" or "sure thing" language
✅ Focus on process, not outcomes

## HOW TO USE THIS FRAMEWORK

### Step 1: Customization
Replace the bracketed placeholders with YOUR business specifics:
- Replace [Core Topic] with your actual topic
- Replace [Pillar 1/2/3] with your educational categories
- Replace [CTA keyword] with your own lead magnet

### Step 2: Apply Your Brand Voice
- Keep the educational structure
- Adapt language to your tone (professional, casual, etc.)
- Match the examples to your actual product/service

### Step 3: Create Your Content Calendar
- Use the 4-week calendar framework
- Fill in YOUR topics per day
- Swap OLD content for NEW educational content
