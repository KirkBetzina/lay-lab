# THE LAY LAB - STYLE GUIDE

## Brand Colors
- **Primary Accent:** #038 (teal)
- **Backgrounds:** #ECEFF3, #F9FAFB
- **CTA Buttons:** White (#FFFFFF)

## Typography
- **Font Family:** System sans-serif (Segoe UI / Roboto)
- **Style:** Clean, modern, minimal

## Imagery
- Horse racing photos
- Profit screenshots
- White CTA buttons
- Charts/graphs for results

## Content Tone
- Educational, not tip-focused
- "My results" not "your results"
- Responsible gambling vibe
- Community-first

## Visual Templates
- Quick tip graphics (30-60 sec)
- Myth vs Fact cards
- Profit result screenshots
- Before/after bankroll growth
- Selection cards (B / M / B+M codes)

## Platform Specs
- **X/Twitter:** 280 char limit, thread-friendly
- **TikTok:** Short-form, fast hooks
- **Instagram:** Reels + Carousels
