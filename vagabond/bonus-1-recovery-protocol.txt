# THE RECOVERY PROTOCOL
## 15-Minute Mobility Routine for Men Who've Sat All Day

---

### WHEN TO USE THIS:
- Before your main workout (5-10 min warmup)
- After your main workout (5-10 min cooldown)
- On rest days as standalone movement
- When you've been sitting for hours (travel, desk, flights)

---

### THE ROUTINE (Complete in Order)

#### 1. Hip Flexor Release (3 min)
**Why:** Sitting destroys hip flexors. This releases the tightness that kills your mobility.

**Movement:**
- Kneeling lunge stretch — 45 seconds each side
- Couch stretch hold — 45 seconds each side
- Hip circles — 20 each direction

#### 2. Thoracic Spine Extension (3 min)
**Why:** Sitting rounds your upper back. This opens the chest and restores posture.

**Movement:**
- Cat-cow flow — 10 reps
- Thoracic rotation — 10 each side (knees on ground, arm extended)
- Wall angels — 10 reps (stand against wall)

#### 3. Shoulder Mobility (3 min)
**Why:** Forward-rounded shoulders from screens and steering wheels.

**Movement:**
- Arm circles — 20 forward, 20 backward
- Cross-body stretch — 30 seconds each arm
- Band pull-aparts or doorway stretch — 20 reps

#### 4. Lower Back Release (3 min)
**Why:** Lower back pain comes from underuse followed by overuse.

**Movement:**
- Child's pose — 45 seconds
- Knee to chest — 30 seconds each side
- Bird-dog — 10 each side (slow, controlled)

#### 5. Neck & Jaw Release (3 min)
**Why:** Stress accumulates here. Most men don't even realize how tight they are.

**Movement:**
- Neck circles — 10 each direction
- Jaw stretch (open wide, hold 10 sec, repeat 5x)
- Chin tucks — 15 reps (pull head straight back)

---

### QUICK VERSION (5 Minutes)
If you're short on time, do only:
1. Hip flexor release (90 sec)
2. Cat-cow (60 sec)
3. Neck release (90 sec)

---

### NOTES
- No equipment needed
- Hotel room, apartment floor, or outdoor space works
- Do in workout clothes or bare feet
- Best done barefoot on non-slip surface
- Repeat daily for best results
