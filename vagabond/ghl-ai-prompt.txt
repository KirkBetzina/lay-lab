9. **GUARANTEE**:
   "Complete all 7 days. Log your proof. If you don't feel a real shift — email me, no questions, full refund."

10. **CTA**:

11. **FAQ** (expand answers):

    - "I've tried programs before and quit" → Address the "quit before" objection directly

- Minimal graphics — keep it gritty and grounded

**Tone:** Masculine, grounded, no hype. Direct and practical.
