### WHAT IS THE IDENTITY THERMOSTAT?

Your Identity Thermostat is the temperature of who you believe yourself to be.

- Low temp: "I'm the guy who quits"
- High temp: "I'm the guy who executes"

Every action you log is evidence that raises your temperature. Every skipped day is evidence that lowers it.

This workbook makes the proof visible.
