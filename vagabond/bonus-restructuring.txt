# Bonus Stack Restructuring - Vagabond 7-Day

---

## The Problem

Your current bonuses:
1. Vagabond Commons (learning + private group)
2. The Vagabond Mindset
3. Living on $20/Day Anywhere
4. Dating Abroad for Men
5. Digital Work on the Road

**Why it kills conversion:**
- Core offer = 7-day fitness/identity reset
- Bonuses = expat lifestyle starter kit
- Prospect confusion: "Is this a fitness program or a travel program?"
- Signals scope creep — buyer thinks "this guy is scattering his focus, how good can the main product be?"

---

## The Fix: Two Paths

### Path A: Keep It Simple (Core Product Only)

Remove the expat bonuses entirely from the main page. Replace with bonuses that serve the core promise (fitness + identity):

**New Bonus Stack:**

1. **The Recovery Protocol** — 15-minute mobility routine for guys who've sit all day. No equipment, hotel-room friendly. (fits mobility/nomad lifestyle actually)

2. **The Bad-Day Mindset Guide** — What to do when motivation crashes. One-page PDF with the mental framework for pushing through when you want to quit. (fits identity shift)

3. **The Identity Thermostat Workbook** — Fill-in-the-blanks system for tracking your daily proof. Makes the logging mechanic tangible. (your unique mechanism)

4. **Private Community Access** — Group for accountability. Even just "ask me questions directly" counts. (fits accountability gap from problem section)

**Why this works:**
- Every bonus connects directly to completing the 7-day program
- No confusion about what the product is
- The $20/day, dating, digital work stuff can exist — but as an UPSELL later, not on the landing page

---

### Path B: Two-Tier Offer (Main + Upsell)

This is better long-term if you want to build a "Vagabond Lifestyle" brand:

**Tier 1 — The Core Program ($27):**
- 7-Day Mobility Protocol
- Proof-Logging System
- Identity Thermostat Framework
- Recovery Protocol
- Bad-Day Mindset Guide

**Tier 2 — Vagabond Lifestyle Bundle (Upsell at $47 or $67):**
- Living on $20/Day Anywhere
- Dating Abroad for Men
- Digital Work on the Road
- Vagabond Commons (full access)

**How to present:**
On the page, show Tier 1 clearly. Then at checkout or as a "one-time offer" after purchase, present Tier 2.

The messaging: "You've got the foundation. Now here's the full Vagabond lifestyle if you want to take it on the road."

---

## Recommended Approach

For right now — **Path A** is fastest to implement:

1. Strip out the expat bonuses from the landing page
2. Replace with the 4 fitness/identity bonuses above
3. Run the $27 offer clean
4. Later, if you want to build the expat brand, create a separate page and funnel for that

This keeps your positioning tight: **7-day fitness + identity reset for men who want to rebuild themselves.**
