# Guarantee Draft - Vagabond 7-Day

---

## Option A: The No-Questions Guarantee (recommended)

### Short version (for under the CTA button):

> "Complete all 7 days. Log your proof. If you don't feel a real shift in how you move and how you see yourself — email me, no questions asked, every penny back."

### Expanded version (for the guarantee section on the page):

**Our Guarantee**

Look, at $27 I'm not asking you to bet your rent on this.

But I am asking you to bet 7 days of your time. That's it.

Here's the deal: complete the full 7-day program. Do the workouts. Log your proof. If at the end you don't feel a shift — not dramatic, just a real change in how your body moves or how you see yourself — email me.

You'll get a full refund within 24 hours. No forms. No hoops. No hard feelings.

You either do the work and get the result, or you get your money back. That's as fair as it gets.

---

## Option B: The Conditional Guarantee (higher bar)

> "Complete all 7 days, log at least 4 days of proof, and if you don't feel measurably better — sleep quality, energy levels, or confidence — email me for a full refund. No questions."

*This one filters for people who actually try, which reduces refund abuse.*

---

## Option C: The Identity Guarantee (more emotional, ties to brand)

> "I built this for guys who've tried everything and quit. This is for the man who starts on Monday and by Wednesday is already off track.
>
> If that's you — and you genuinely complete the 7 days — you'll know by Day 7 that something has shifted. If it hasn't, I'll refund you. No debate.
>
> I'm not worried about the refund. I'm worried about the guy who quits on Day 3 and never gives himself the chance to prove his identity can change."

---

## Placement Notes

- Put it directly under the price/CTA button
- Use it again in the FAQ: "What if I don't like it?" → link to guarantee
- The guarantee is a closer — it's worth as much as a paragraph of sales copy

---

## Why This Works

At $27:
- Almost nobody will ask for a refund (low-ticket psychology)
- It removes the single biggest barrier to purchase: "what if it doesn't work?"
- It signals full confidence in the product
- Dan Kennedy said: the guarantee is where you either believe in your product or you don't — and the prospect can tell
