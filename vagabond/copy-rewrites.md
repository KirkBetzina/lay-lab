## 11. FAQ

**Current:** One-liners, missed closers.

**Rewritten:**

"FAQ — Answered:

**Q: What if I'm out of shape?**
A: Perfect. This system was built for the man who's completely deconditioned. Day 1 starts with a 20-minute mobility workout — if you can't do it all, do part of it. The proof comes from starting, not from perfection.

**Q: I've tried programs before and quit. Why is this different?**
A: Most programs ask you to believe first. This one asks you to act first. You don't need motivation — you need one rep. Then proof. Then another rep. The identity builds from the evidence, not the emotion.

**Q: How is this different from just doing YouTube workouts?**
A: YouTube gives you content. This system gives you a proof loop. There's no tracking, no accountability, no identity shift. You finish a YouTube video and nothing is recorded. This system records every action — that's what changes you.

**Q: Do I need equipment or a gym?**
A: No. The entire mobility protocol is designed for no equipment. Hotel room, apartment, park — anywhere works.

**Q: What if I don't like it?**
A: Complete all 7 days, log your proof, and if you don't feel a shift — email me for a full refund. No forms. No hoops. 100% guaranteed."

**Why it works:**
- Each answer is 2-3 sentences, not one word
- Addresses "I've quit before" objection explicitly

- Links back to guarantee
