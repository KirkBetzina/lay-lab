### WELCOME TO THE INNER CIRCLE

You're here because you committed to the work. This is your accountability hub.

### WHAT'S INSIDE

- **Private group access** — Where all Vagabond members connect
- **Direct line to Kirk** — Ask questions, get feedback
- **Weekly check-in threads** — Stay consistent
- **Win sharing** — Celebrate progress, get pushed when you stall
- **Member-only discussions** — Real conversations, real men

---

### HOW TO USE THIS COMMUNITY

**1. Introduce Yourself**
Post in the introduction thread:
- Your name
- Where you're based
- What you're working on (fitness, identity, discipline)
- One goal for the next 7 days

**2. Check In Weekly**
Every Sunday, there's a check-in thread. Reply with:
- What you completed
- What you missed
- What you're committing to next week

**3. Ask Questions**
Got a hurdle? Post it. Kirk responds within 24-48 hours. Other members often have solutions too.

**4. Share Wins**
Finished a workout? Logged your proof? Had a breakthrough? Share it. The community celebrates every win.

---

### GROUND RULES

- No drama
- No politics
- No spam
- Support, don't judge
- Keep it masculine and grounded

This is a space for men doing the work. Not for spectators.

---

### GETTING THE MOST OUT OF IT

**Lurking won't help you.**
Post. Engage. Share. The more you put in, the more you get out.

**Accountability works both ways.**
Hold others accountable. Expect others to hold you accountable.

**Bad days happen.**
Post when you fail. Don't hide it. The community supports men who keep showing up — not men who pretend they're perfect.
