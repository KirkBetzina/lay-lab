# Testimonial Request Script - Vagabond 7-Day Beta

---

## Option 1: Quick Text (for follow-up after they complete Day 7)

**Subject:** Quick Q re: your week

Hey [Name],

Congrats on finishing the 7 days. I know that wasn't easy.

One quick thing — can you send me 2-3 sentences on what changed for you? Doesn't have to be fancy. Something like what you noticed in your body, your energy, your mindset. Even a small shift matters.

I want to put together some case studies for the page, and honestly your experience is the real proof.

Thanks,
Kirk

---

## Option 2: Mid-Program Check-In (Day 3-4)

**Subject:** How's the week going?

Hey [Name],

Checking in — how are you feeling on Day [X]?

Two things:
1. Any hurdles come up? (I want to know what's hard so I can fix it)
2. What have you noticed so far? (Even small things — better sleep, more energy, whatever)

Drop me a reply when you can. Genuinely interested in what's happening for you.

— Kirk

---

## Option 3: Post-Program Request (more detailed)

**Subject:** Your results → featured?

[Name],

Completed the 7 days. Respect.

Here's what I'm thinking: I want to feature real guys and their real results on the landing page. Not actors, not stock photos. Just the truth.

If you're willing, I'd love to get:
- Before/after (photo or just description)
- 2-3 sentences on what changed
- First name + city (you pick what goes public)

If you'd rather stay anonymous, just first name or even just "M" works.

No pressure either way. But if it helped, other guys deserve to know.

Let me know,
Kirk

---

## Tips for Getting Responses

1. Ask on Day 3-4 while they're in the middle — momentum is high, they're engaged
2. Ask again at Day 7 right after they finish — fresh in their mind
3. Make it easy — don't ask for long paragraphs, 2-3 sentences is plenty
4. Be specific in your ask — "what did you notice in your body?" beats "how was it?"
5. Offer anonymity — removes hesitation for private guys

---

## Example Responses You Can Prompt Them With

If they hesitate, give them a template:
> "Honestly I felt like shit most days but by day 5 I noticed I was sleeping better and waking up without hitting snooze. That alone told me something was shifting."

> "I lost 5 lbs but honestly the bigger thing was my mood. I wasn't snapping at my coworkers anymore."

> "I'm not gonna lie — day 2 I wanted to quit. But I stuck with it and by day 7 I actually looked forward to the workouts. First time that's happened in years."

Get them to tell the truth, even if it's messy. That's what sells.
